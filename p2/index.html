Chewin’ the Fat

w/ Jay Howell

by, Josh Yule

  • BEFORE

    Fave 90’s indie rock record?

    I was going to say the Pixies surfer Rosa but that was 1988. I'll still say that. It had a huge impact on me as a young person.

    Simon Larbalestier

  • DIGITAL

    Did you ever think that your work would make it to the digital realm? Was it a tough transitional process or did you adapt moderately?

    I was never thinking of the digital realm. I was thinking about getting a book published or making a comic. For some reason I never thought I could or would do digital stuff. But every time I'd draw I would imagine it moving and talking in my head. Once I saw my art animated for the first time I had a major revelation! I'm a slow learner.

    Blogspot

  • SKATE

    What 80’s skateboard deck boasts your fave art? Do most consider it iconic art or is it just nostalgic for you?

    My Favorite skateboard deck of all time is maybe the Rob Roskopp series from Jim Phillips. He's really my favorite skate artist ever. I got teary when I met him.

    BIG ugly

  • MEDIUMS

    From an outsider's perspective you seem to have teetered between the physical and digital output of your work nicely. Any words or attributes of encouragement for the next generation of illustrators making this leap?

    I would always say it's a good idea to keep up on the digital trends. I got into programs like illustrator and photoshop because I wanted to be in control of the final product. If you just send your artwork into some company for a graphic you never know what you're going to get. I also love the crispy cleanness of computers. you can really get microscopic when drawing with a Wacom tablet.

    how to...

  • BOB'S BURGERS

    I recall the time when you first started working on the character development for Bob’s Burgers and it was an exciting life event for you, us too by the way! Do you still get super stoked on new projects or has that feeling manifested into more of a humbling experience?

    It's always the same exciting feeling. I love new projects and go into them full force. I try and not be jaded. I mean, I get to draw for a living which is insane.

    Character Development

  • FOOD

    I see you like to wrestle with fine dining and new trendy restaurants on social media. Do you still just order a cheap late night pizza and listen to choice tunes with buddys? Fave 90’s indie rock record?

    I do love fine dining, but I love to eat everywhere in Los Angeles. I've been here 11 years and i'm still finding out about so much cool food. I love cheap pizza and choice tunes! I'm the same fun idiot i've always have been.

    Mc Rad

  • DRUMS

    You put your music career in the rearview mirror, you ever just spot a drum set from across the room and wanna hop on for a ride?

    I think about drumming everyday. I'm heading up to Sacramento next weekend and i'm gonna go jam with some old buds. I like to work alone a lot, being in a band is a nasty little scene.

    SHOVE/Bead artihmetic
